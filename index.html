Rock, Paper or Scissors?

ROCK

Why choose rock? The rock is a powerful weapon that smashes the scissors into small bits. A piece of paper will make the rock tremble with fear.

PAPER

Why choose paper? Paper is an assassin that wraps the strong rock in and suffocates it. Paper is fragile and will tear if a sharp scissor touches it.

SCISSORS

Why choose scissors? The scissors are sharp enough to cut through any type of paper, but as much as a little smack on the top will shatter it into shards of metal.
